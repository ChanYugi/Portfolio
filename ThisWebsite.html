This Very Website!
Hi! Here, I can explain a little bit more about this website, as well as some of the processes and problems I had to solve along the way.
Originally, when I wanted to build my own website, I stated looking for inspiration on the general experience or feeling that it gives off when someone browses through it. I looked at some student examples, a few portfolios, but I took a lot of inspiration from the Google Chrome download page. Yes... the download page.
Navigation
I noticed that in a lot of the student examples and professional portfolios, they were typically missing navigation features. I found myself clicking buttons and links to other pages that rarely had a thoughtful way to return to the main page. So, I decided to make this aspect a main feature within my website.
This is where Google Chrome comes in. Their download page has a main section with different navigation options and a sticky banner that appears whenever the main section goes out of the viewport. I thought that they had a clever solution that was seamless and allowed for excellent navigation.
